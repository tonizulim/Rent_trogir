THINGS TO SEE & DO

Places to See Near Trogir


Beach bar Borko


Located just 15 minutes from Trogir, Beach Bar Borko is a must-visit coastal bar on Čiovo island. Ideal for scooter travelers looking to relax by the beach, Borko offers great cocktails, chill music, and amazing sea views. This beach bar is a perfect stop when renting a scooter in Trogir and exploring Čiovo.


View on Map

Gospa od Prizidnice


This serene cliffside monastery is located just 20 minutes from Trogir. Gospa od Prizidnice is one of the most spiritual and breathtaking spots on Čiovo Island. Ideal for travelers with scooters who enjoy a peaceful view and historical landmarks. It’s one of the hidden gems best discovered on two wheels.

View on Map | Reviews

Beach Okrug


Located just 10 minutes from Trogir, Okrug Beach is one of the most popular beaches in the area. Easily reachable with a 50cc or 125cc scooter, it offers a mix of lively beach bars, water sports, and a long stretch of pebbly beach perfect for a relaxing scooter day trip.

30-Minute Scooter Trips


Marina Tower & Town


Marina is a charming seaside town just 30 minutes from Trogir, perfect for scooter adventures. Visit the iconic Marina Tower, enjoy seaside cafés, and explore quiet coastal roads. It’s a great scooter-friendly day trip destination near Trogir.

View on Map | Reviews

Kaštela – Old Town & Kaštilac Fortress


Explore the historic towns of Kaštela, especially the beautiful Kaštilac Fortress featured in Game of Thrones. About 30 minutes from Trogir, it’s a perfect place to explore by scooter, with lovely coastal routes and charming Dalmatian architecture.

View on Map | Reviews

Rogoznica & Zmajevo Oko


Rogoznica is a beautiful coastal village about 35 minutes from Trogir. It's perfect for a longer scooter ride along the scenic Adriatic coast. While you're there, don't miss Zmajevo Oko (Dragon’s Eye), a natural saltwater lake with stunning views and fascinating legends. A great day trip option, especially with a 125cc or 250cc scooter.

Must-See Places Farther Out (NOTE: ONLY FOR 125cc scooter or bigger)


Monument to Our Lady of Loreto


Located about 30 minutes from Trogir, this massive hilltop statue near Primošten offers panoramic views of the Adriatic coastline. A scenic and inspiring destination best accessed with a 125cc or 250cc scooter for a smooth uphill ride and coastal road experience.

View on Map | Reviews

Split – Diocletian’s Palace


Split is a vibrant coastal city and home to the world-famous Diocletian’s Palace, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Please note: Split is not recommended for 50cc scooters due to highway access and traffic.

View on Map | Reviews

Klis Fortress


Famous for its Game of Thrones filming location, Klis Fortress offers stunning views over Split and the coastline. Due to its hilltop location, Klis is not recommended for small scooters (50cc). A must-see if you're on a longer scooter adventure!
